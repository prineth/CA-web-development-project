The Knuckles Mountain Range lies in central Sri Lanka, in the Districts of Matale and Kandy. In addition to its aesthetic value, the range is of great scientific interest. It is a climatic microcosm of the rest of Sri Lanka as the conditions of all the climatic zones in the country are exhibited in the massif.
