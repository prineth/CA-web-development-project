Pidurangala is a gigantic rock located a few kilometres north of the famous Sigiriya rock fortress. According to history, whilst King Kasyapa was building Sigiriya Rock Fortress in the 5th century, he is said to have moved monks living around Sigiriya to a new monastery on Pidurangala
